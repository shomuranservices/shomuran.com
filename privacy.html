Privacy Policy for CardCompass

Effective Date: December 1, 2025


Shomuran Services LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the CardCompass mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

CardCompass does not collect or store any personal, financial, or sensitive user data. The app works locally on your device and does not connect to any external financial accounts or servers.

2. Information We May Collect (Optional Use)

If you choose to enable location access, CardCompass uses your device's location only to detect nearby merchants and improve reward suggestions. This information never leaves your device.

3. Data Storage

All data, including card details or preferences you enter, are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or share this information externally.

4. Third-Party Services

CardCompass does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or data tracking tools.

5. Security

We take your privacy seriously. All information is processed on your device and never shared with our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

CardCompass is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at https://shomuran.com/privacy.
